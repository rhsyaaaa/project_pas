What is a VPN server?

A VPN server is a protected remote server that forwards your data securely over the internet. It's a unique combination of proprietary hardware and software, making it much more sophisticated than a simple remote server. VPN servers can be further customized for specific tasks, such as P2P file sharing or Tor access.

VPN server infrastructure is critical to your security, privacy, and comfort online. For example, servers close to your actual location often guarantee the best connection speed and streaming quality. This is why it is important to have a variety of the latest VPN servers spread across the world
